Persian

Everyone knows the Persian cat, which is famous for its fur, expressive eyes and the extension of its face. The Persian is also one of the oldest cat breeds. It is calm and loving gently and loves a calm environment. The Persian needs to be combed and decorated daily because of the length of his hair.

Maine Coon

This beautiful cat is one of the largest breeds of domestic cats, and the Maine Coon takes three to four years To reach full physical maturity, she has a very thick fur with a waterproof layer, has wide and large paws to make it suitable for walking on the snow.

Siberian cat

The origin of the Siberian cat is due to the Russian mountains, and it is characterized by many characteristics, including thick and beautiful hair and multicoloredness, where we find brown, cream, silver and red, and the color of the eyes may be green, blue or golden,

british shorthair

These lovely cats have thick, sturdy looking bodies, round heads, and soft blue-gray fur. Their calm personality and low energy levels make them great companion choices for first-time cat owners. British Shorthair cats like to eat a lot, so you should feed them two or three meals throughout the day rather than giving them a large bowl to eat in their spare time.

ragdoll cat

The Ragdoll cat has a very distinctive shape from the rest of the other breeds, as it is characterized by blue eyes and thick white fur that complements its appearance. These cats require brushing their teeth from time to time, and combing their thick hair twice a week to protect it from microbes.
