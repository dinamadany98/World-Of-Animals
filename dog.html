Papillon dog

It is a medium-sized dog with thick hair and black and round eyes.
It is of French origin. The Papillon dog is one of the pet dogs that you can raise at home, as it is considered one of the dogs that responds to the orders of its breeder and implements them.

Bolognese dogs

Bolognese dogs originated in Italy and are considered one of the pet dogs that you can raise at home, where they do not exhibit aggressive behavior, but on the contrary are very friendly with children and love to have fun and are distinguished

Keeshond

A large local breed, which brought the Eskimo tribes to work in skiing. Alaskan Malamutes do not bark, but communicate by grumbling and howling. But this is not the only advantage - dogs need regular physical exertion and are not suitable for everyone
